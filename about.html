About me

Introduction

I am enrolled in a BA (Hons) program in Visual Communications and Design at South East Technological University, and I also hold a BTEC Level 3 Diploma in Creative Media Production and a Professional Diploma in Graphic Design.

Over the past three years of studying Visual Communications, I have acquired various skills, including proficiency in Figma, Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, and XD, as well as web-based languages including HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, and MySQL. Additionally, I have experience with Balsamiq and Figma. I have also gained foundational knowledge through an Introduction to Marketing, Marketing Mix, and Marketing Management.

Interests and hobbies

I have a strong interest in design and technology, and I aim to pursue a career in digital design, UI/UX, or web and app design and development. In my free time, I am passionate about and have experience in PC and console gaming.
